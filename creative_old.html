Who else am I
"I want to create immersive worlds that can be a means of escape from reality through art and code."

I've been a gamer since I could hold a controller. That obsession didn't stay passive — I started modding, then designing, then building. Games taught me that the most powerful systems are the ones people don't notice — they just feel.

The same instinct drives the book. Broke-Stalker is a neo-noir psychological thriller — the kind of story I wanted to read and couldn't find. So I'm writing it.

Alongside that: logo design, poster work, illustration, video editing. I believe design and code are the same conversation — both are about shaping how something is experienced.
